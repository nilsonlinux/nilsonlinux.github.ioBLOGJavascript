Sobre Nilsonlinux

Melograf Mastering is operated by engineer Christopher Leary. A keen musician, Leary has been writing and producing music since the late nineties, developing a reputation for pristine production and attention to detail.

Since 2007, he has mastered over five hundred projects for independent labels and artists. A handful of his growing mastering credits can be seen on Discogs.com.

Enjoying the combination of objective technical precision and creative musical shaping that the mastering process affords, Leary decided to launch a mastering business in earnest, establishing Melograf Mastering in the late summer of 2009.
